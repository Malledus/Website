The Last Day

The last day of 40/40 is the single most important day of the event. It represents the fact that you have completed the challenge of running 1 mile every day for 40 days. On the last day of 40/40, every student and teacher, that has still fulfilled their commitment to running a mile a day, come together for the final mile and run it together. While many people sign up each year, those numbers dwindle out by the end of 40/40, but each year more and more people are completing 40/40. On the last day, everyone that has completed 40/40 so far go down to the track at USJ to complete the last mile. Last year, we took a picture to commerate the accomplishment which is pictured above. You are also entered into a lottery to receive a gift card for a free pair of running shoes if you complete it so there is some monetary incentive behind completing 40/40. Of course, the main idea is behind establishing a good habit and better yourself and that is what the last day signifies.
